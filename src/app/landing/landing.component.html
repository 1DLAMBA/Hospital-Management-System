How Appointments Work

Sign up and browse our network of licensed doctors and specialists. Select a doctor, pick an available time slot, and confirm your booking. You will receive a notification once your appointment is confirmed, and reminders as the date approaches.

Real-Time Doctor Communication

Message your doctor directly through Phoenix with our built-in chat. Send and receive messages in real time, track delivery and read receipts, and stay informed with instant notifications for replies and appointment updates.
