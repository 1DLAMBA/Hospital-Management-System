How to use Phoenix for your healthcare needs

Book an Appointment

Browse our network of available doctors and specialists. Select a doctor based on their specialty and availability, choose a time slot that works for you, and confirm your booking. You will receive a notification once your appointment is confirmed, along with reminders as the date approaches.

Access Medical Records

Your complete medical history lives in one secure place. View past visits, prescriptions, diagnoses, and doctor notes at any time. Share your records with any doctor on the platform so they have the full picture before your appointment, saving time and improving the quality of your care.

AI Health Assistant

Have a health question at any hour? Our AI assistant is available around the clock to help you understand symptoms, get guided recommendations on which specialist to see, and prepare for your next appointment with relevant insights. Think of it as a knowledgeable companion on your health journey.

Message Your Doctor

Chat directly with your healthcare provider through Phoenix in real time. Ask follow-up questions after a visit, share updates on how you are feeling, or clarify instructions. Track message delivery and read receipts, and receive notifications whenever your doctor replies or your appointment status changes.

Your Dashboard

Everything you need is on your personal dashboard. View upcoming appointments, recent medical records, and unread notifications all in one place. Manage your profile, update your health information, and track your complete healthcare journey on Phoenix from a single screen.
